Scots - Motor Scooters - Site Plan

Site Name

Scots - Motor Scooters

Site Purpose

The purpose of Scoots motor scooters is to offer to their clients a trustable renting option for scooters, ATVs, and jeeps in Cozumel Island, Mexico. The page must highlight the island's attractiveness, display what type of vehicles are offered, and make it possible for the user to reserve their desired vehicles. In addition, the page must use a palette of colors inspired by the Caribbean region.

Scenarios

  • Who are the posible clients?
  • What kind of services are offered?
  • What are company's officies locations?
  • What kind of vehicles does the company offers?
