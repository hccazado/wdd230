Rosario Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

Rosario's Chamber of Commerce is a leading non-governmental organization that leads Rosario's associated businesses to strengthen their relationship with each other and the community by promoting publicity, and public events. In addition, the organization seeks to help the citizens to develop a better social environment, thus uplifting local commerce.
